Mechatronic Design Project
The team was tasked to develop a mechatronic system in three weeks that would interface with an X-Y gantry to identify magnets and place a plastic cup on each.
A mechanical design which could collect, store and place cups without modification was sketched.
To locate magnets, the design scanned across the area and gathered data representing the magnetic field strength using a hall-effect sensor. This was processed and the magnets were located using MATLAB.
Each of the actuators and sensors were controlled by a bespoke shield for the Arduino Mega.
The final design scanned the area using a hall-effect sensor, collecting cups using a DC motor closed-loop controlled by a continuous potentiometer. Vertical actuation was achieved using a stepper motor, with stalls being detected by a light-gate acting as a simple encoder.
